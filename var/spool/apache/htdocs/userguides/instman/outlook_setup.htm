• Open Outlook Click On Tools Then Accounts
  • Click On Add
  • Select The Mail Option
  • Enter The Name You Want Displayed To Recipients
  • Click Next
  • Enter Your Full Email Address As Configured On The Firewall
  • Click Next
  • Select Your Service Type Both Will Operate.
  • POP3 Is The Preferred Protocol But Mail May Not Be Left On The Server
  • Enter The Address Of The Firewall For Both Incoming And Outgoing Mail Servers This Is The Full Address Hostname And Domain Ie firewall.company.co.za
  • Click Next 
  • Enter Your User Name And Password For This Email Address
  • Do Not Enable Secure Password Authentication
  • Click Next
  • As You Are On A Network Select Connect Using LAN
  • Click Next
  • Click Finish
  • Select The New Account From The Account List And Click Properties
  • Click The Servers tab
  • Enable "My Server Requires Authentication"
  • Click On Settings
  • Ensure "Use Same Settings As My Incoming Server Is Ticked"
  • Click Ok
  • Click On Advanced
  • Enable SSL For Both Incoming And Outgoing Mail Servers
  • Click Ok
  • Click Ok Again To Return To Outlook
  • Click Send And Receive
